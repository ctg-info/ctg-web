About

CTG-Informática explores problems at the frontier of knowledge in computing and digital systems. We work where answers are still emerging, testing bold ideas, challenging assumptions, and turning uncertainty into insight.

Our culture is open, creative, and rigorous. We treat conjecture as a starting point, experimentation as method, and error correction as a core strength. By combining research, knowledge transfer, integration, and innovation, we develop solutions with global relevance and practical impact in Colombia.

Research Areas

  • Quantum Computing & Computational Theory
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Data Management & Smart Territories
  • Software Engineering for AI/ML
  • Digital Transformation
  • Cybersecurity (Emerging Line)

Projects

Development and Implementation of the ICT Platform for the Cundinamarca Agro-Industrial Technological Corridor Strategy (CTA-2) (2026)

Contracted by the Government of Cundinamarca. Components: (1) Learning Management System (LMS), (2) digital pedagogical content, and (3) a virtual space for service promotion.

SISVida RAG Platform (2025-2026)

AI-assisted platform for auditing and traceability in healthcare claims processes.

El Quimbo Hydropower Oxygenation Optimization (2022-2026)

Intelligent control and sustainable technology implementation to improve dissolved oxygen processes in hydropower operations.

Digital Transformation of EMGESA Renewable Generation Plants (2021-2024)

Research and development project focused on improving reliability and efficiency in operation, maintenance, and management processes for renewable generation assets.

Reproducibility and Knowledge Transfer in Large-Scale Scientific Experiments (2023)

Project on reproducible computational science, distributed infrastructure, and portable execution environments for research workflows.

Software Repository Mining with Machine Learning (2022)

Applied research to extract engineering insights from code and issue repositories using data analytics and machine learning methods.

New Paradigms for Systems-of-Systems and Smart Cities Architecture (2021-2022)

Research on complex systems design, enterprise architecture, and knowledge-based approaches for large-scale information systems and smart territories.

CAP4CITY - Strengthening Governance Capacity for Smart Sustainable Cities (2018-2022)

Erasmus+ project with international partners to develop training and governance capabilities for smart sustainable cities.

Reactive Middleware for Big Data Processing (2018)

Design and development of reactive programming tools for distributed middleware handling large-scale data processing.

Cloud and Mobile Distributed Enterprise Architectures (2017-2018)

Research and prototyping of architectural patterns to support enterprise solutions deployed across distributed cloud and mobile infrastructures.

Framework for e-Health and m-Health Service Solutions (2017)

Framework-oriented project to accelerate design and implementation of software-based health service solutions.
